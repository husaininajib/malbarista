About Us

malbarian made coffee shop

Malbarista Book Cafe is a local brand coffee house that
promotes delicious coffee and a relaxing vibe. This shop
serves Malabarian cultural foods and variety options of
mouth-watering drinks that could delight all five senses
with lively surroundings, smooth music, and aromatic,
delicious brews.

The name Malbarista was derived from a combination
of the word ‘Malbarian’ which is our community,
and ‘Barista’ which is a person who serves in a coffee bar.
So the word ‘Malbarista’ was actually meant as
“Coffee prepared by a Malbarian''.
